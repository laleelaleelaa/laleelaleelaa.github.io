a public sanitary pavilion in the Citadelpark
Location: Gent, Belgium
Start: 2023


www.ensemble.gent
A proposal for an architectural pavilion for the Citadelpark of Ghent which includes a new (and first) genderneutral, wheelchair accessible public toilet offering a covered public space for resting, playing and meeting. Fully reusable in materiality, without leaving a trace on the protected surroundings.

In collaboration with Marine Boey, Fred Meeuwens, Wolf Vandekerckhove and Natan Van Laethem.
