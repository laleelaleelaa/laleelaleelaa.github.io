CURRENT OCCUPATION

As the founding member of studio 1/1, Lina is currently working as an architect wihtin this young designers collective. Alongside her architectural practice at studio 1/1, she is also active as a freelance architect at Coussée Goris Huyghe architecten.

Beside her full-time occupation as an architect, Lina takes a great interest in the craft of small objects. Objects not quite of essential need, but which are of great emotional value and are driven to create from a deep passion for beauty and craftsmanship.

Additionally, Lina enjoys exploring her fascinations in other fields of art and design.
